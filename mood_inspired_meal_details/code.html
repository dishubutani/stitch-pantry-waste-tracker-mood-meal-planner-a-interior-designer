Berry &
Walnut Oats

Designed for clarity and sustained cognitive focus. A morning ritual that nourishes your mind as much as your body.

The Focus Engine

Walnuts are rich in DHA, a type of Omega-3 fatty acid shown to protect brain health in newborns, improve cognitive performance in adults, and prevent or slow age-related cognitive decline.

shutter_speed

Antioxidant Boost

Blueberries contain anthocyanins, which cross the blood-brain barrier and enhance signaling in brain centers.

Ingredients

  • Steel-cut Oats 1/2 cup
  • Toasted Walnuts 1/4 cup
  • Fresh Blueberries 1/2 cup
  • Chia Seeds 1 tbsp
  • Manuka Honey 1 tsp

Preparation Steps

01
Slow Simmer

Combine oats with 1.5 cups of water or nut milk. Simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes until creamy. Patience develops the best texture.

02
Antioxidant Infusion

Fold in the chia seeds and half of the blueberries while the oats are still hot. This allows the berries to slightly burst and release their juices.

03
The Brain Finish

Top with the remaining fresh berries and raw toasted walnuts. Drizzle honey in a circular motion to finish the editorial presentation.
